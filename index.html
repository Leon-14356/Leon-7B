The Champions League Trophy


We have covered a lot of how the Champions League works and some of the history but now we are going to talk about the actual trophy.
The Champions League trophy has two types of trophies. The trophy that players get to lift and celebrate is the replacement trophy which is made of sterling silver and is 73.5cm tall and weighs 7.5kg and has a monetary
value estimated to be worth BHD 5,650 or if your in america that is $15,000. The actual trophy of the champions league although, is estimated to be 1.62 metres tall and roughly
weighs 45kg. But the truly extraordinarily and fascinating fact about the Champions League is that it is worth a whoppin $1.3 million!. Even though the Champions League trophy is the silverware that everyone
wants to win, it's not the only thing you can win as their is also a gold medal for winning it as well(made of a partial amount of gold). The Champions League medal consists of sterling
silver and is also made up of many metal alloys but it's appearance shows as gold as it has a limited amount of it which covers the medal.
The medal in the Champions League can be seen as a little brother to the big silver trophy that every team wants to win so not many people recognise it as it isn't as big or noticeable.
